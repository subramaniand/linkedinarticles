Risk register
Adoption shortfall
Target users don't engage with AI tools. Mitigate with change management, champions programme, and role-specific training.
High likelihood
Data quality gaps
Poor data estate limits model accuracy. Mitigate with pre-investment data audit and a parallel data quality programme.
Medium likelihood
Security & compliance
Data leakage or regulatory breach via AI systems. Mitigate with private endpoints, PII scanning, and governance framework.
Medium likelihood
Cost overrun
Token costs and infrastructure scale beyond projections. Mitigate with usage monitoring, budget alerts, and pre-paid tiers once stable.
Lower likelihood
Hallucination & quality
AI outputs are incorrect or misleading. Mitigate with RAG grounding, human-in-the-loop review, and output testing frameworks.
Medium likelihood
Vendor lock-in
Over-dependence on a single platform creates switching risk. Mitigate with open standards (MCP, OpenAI API spec) and multi-vendor contracts.
Lower likelihood
